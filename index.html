Discover Our Story

Our Legacy

Established in 1958 by Sri Damodaran Vaidyar at Karaparamba, Kozhikode, Valiyeri Ayurveda stands on a rich heritage of authentic Ayurvedic tradition. Sri Damodaran Vaidyar hailed from a renowned family of physicians, carrying forward generations of Ayurvedic wisdom.

In 1980, a dedicated medicine manufacturing unit was established at Narikkuni, Kozhikode, to support the vaidyasala. The unit maintains its own herbal garden to ensure the purity and quality of raw materials, while rare medicinal herbs are traditionally sourced from forest regions through trusted indigenous communities.
